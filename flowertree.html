flowertree

Grow mindfulness right from your phone! Through daily check-ins, journal entries, and soundscapes, FlowerTree is there to help you become the best version of yourself. It's a loud world out there and for many—it's even louder in their minds.

As the sole UI/UX Designer on the project, I was inspired to create FlowerTree to provide an opportunity for people to implement mindfulness into their daily lives—right from their phones.

Project Goal

Offer valuable and engaging content on mindfulness whilst improving user retention.

Mindfulness has a lot of benefits, such as: increasing focus and attention, managing stress, and nurturing peace within oneself. Yet, so many people don't stick with it.

Design Flow

Research Objectives

1. Find out what features users desire in mindfulness apps.

2. What motivates users to practice mindfulness?

3. What do users like and dislike about mental health trackers?

Domain Research

I compared popular mental health apps and explored the interfaces, as well as reading customer reviews. Some of the apps we investigated were Headspace, Calm, Relax Melodies, My Life, and Meditation & Relaxation to compare features.

Primary Research

After conducting 7 semi-structured user interviews and 52 surveys, I built a collective list of trends and themes mentioned throughout user interviews. I chose current mindfulness users as respondents because I felt they would provide the best insights to what users want out of a mindfulness app.

Secondary Research

In order to understand how to make the use of mindfulness apps more habitual, I implemented the hook model, which is the framework for engaging users to build habit-forming behaviours.

Habit-building Features

Users will be allowed to name a plant that they can water each day through daily check-ins. The more they consistently do these activities, the more the plant will grow. Through this gamification technique, research indicates that users will be more likely to build consistency and habits.

Color Palette

Typography

Future Opportunities

FlowerTree was a rewarding, yet humbling experience to create.

The app provides resources to help people become more aware of their emotions and find stability. Though FlowerTree currently has valuable features to offer, it still has room for further growth.

Future

1. User Testing

Evaluate and gather feedback from a larger selection of users.

2. Guided Meditations

Given the tight timeline, guided meditations were not implemented, but are highly recommended for the future of FlowerTree. It is especially beneficial to have an instructor lead a meditation practice for new users.

3. Focused Topics

Users can choose meditations and activities tailored to their needs.

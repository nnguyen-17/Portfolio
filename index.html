ABOUT

Nancy Nguyen

UI/UX Designer

UI/UX Designer with a vision to bridge humanity and technology together.

I've always been curious about the human mind; how one processes information, makes decisions, and finds fulfillment in the depths of life. It all started when I got together people in town to teach the art of mindfulness— up until the day I downloaded a code editor on my laptop and discovered another medium to foster my passions.

With years of experience understanding the psychology of the human mind and also user design training, I am inspired to dive into the intersection of humanity and technology. With the press of a button, I am determined to improve the user experience to ultimately improve our well-being— all done with a smile.
